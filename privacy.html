Privacy at txtagent

txtagent rewrites text with as little data collection as practical. This product summary is not yet the final legal policy required for publication.

Text you submit

Text is sent to txtagent’s backend and AI provider only after you press Rewrite. txtagent does not save submitted messages or generated rewrites in its database.

Operational information

txtagent stores an anonymous installation identifier, daily usage counts, coarse country and region codes, browser and operating-system families, extension version, language, response timing, and error counts.

Information we avoid

txtagent does not intentionally store raw IP addresses, full user-agent strings, visited URLs, browsing history, submitted messages, generated responses, or personal API keys on its backend.

Third-party processing

Cloudflare provides API and database infrastructure. An AI model provider processes text to generate rewrites. The final policy will identify providers, retention terms, deletion options, and contact details before public release.
